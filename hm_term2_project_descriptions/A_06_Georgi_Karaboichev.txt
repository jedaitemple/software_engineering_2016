InfiNote

Concept:

A note-taking app with an infinite canvas

Features:

- Zoom out whenever you need more space
- You can add:
   - Sketches
   - Titles
   - Headings
   - Photos
   - Check lists
   - Bulleted, numbered and dashed lists
- Everything is stored in the cloud
