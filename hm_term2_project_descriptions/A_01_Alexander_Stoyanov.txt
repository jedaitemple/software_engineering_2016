Kitchen saga 
An  2D action game made with game maker studio.
An evil rat has taken over the kitchen. It has eaten your entire bread/fruit/vegetable family and your the lonely survivor. Avenge your fallen family , defeat the rat and his minions and free the kitchen.
 
Features:
-different levels/maps
-different enemy mobs
-multiple character skins(bread/fruits/vegetables)
-melee combat
-action music
-boss fight
-character progression
