Sudoku Application
A game that is known to a wide variety of people. The application is here to brighten up your day with
limitless collection of randomly generated sudokus and variety of difficulties.

Features:
- Sudoku Generator
  ~ Three difficulty levels
- Tips for the game
- Options
  ~ Screen size 
  ~ Screen colour
  <!- Eventually a music background -!>
- Sudoku Solving Algorithm
- Animating the solving process
