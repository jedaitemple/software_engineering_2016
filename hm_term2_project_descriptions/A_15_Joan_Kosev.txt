The cat and the caterpillar

"The cat and the caterpillar" is a 2D RPG game with unique combat mechanics.
Dive into a great story about the friendship between "Keaur" the cat
and "Stub" the caterpillar, fighting for their survival in the forest.

Features:
-Large diversity of mini games
-Original art
-Hard gameplay based on multitasking
-Audio

Technologies:
-C++
-SDL2
-OpenGL
-OpenGameArt
