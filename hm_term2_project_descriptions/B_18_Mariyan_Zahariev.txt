Addon за WoW. Езика на който ще бъде написан е LUA.

Features:
  - every player item (raid/party group)
  - calculates avarage item level
  - lists the relics of the weapons
  - shows detailed item information (without hovering over it)
  - history of killed raid bosses
  - scans for foodBuff & flaskBuff (raid/party group)
  - history of mithyc + dungeons
  - lists all legendary items (equipped only)
  - arena rating
  - honorable kills
  - rated BG rating
  - artefact traits level
  - quick settings menu
  - key binding optimised system
  - "cooldown ready" message (pops up when a spell can be used again)
